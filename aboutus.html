Who We Are



From its roots as the oldest bank in Binangonan, Rizal, with a mission to extend credit to small farmers and merchants, BRB has now evolved into the most progressive and innovative Rural Bank in the whole of the Philippines.

BRB is the 1st Rural Bank in the Philippines to launch E-Money issuance operations.

Mission

To carry and engage in the business of extending credit to small farmers and merchants and to deserving industries or enterprise. To do and perform all acts and transacts all business which may be done by the Rural Banks in accordance to the Rural Bank's Act.

CASHMATE CASH CARD TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. ABOUT THE CARD. The CASHMATE Cash Card is a Pre-Paid Card issued by Binangonan Rural Bank (“BRB”). It is a prepaid, reloadable, multi-purpose electronic value card, which may be used locally as a cash card for banking and other lawful transactions.

2. REGISTRATION AND ACTIVATION. The Customer is required to completely fill out the Customer Application Form and present a copy of any valid identification. The CASHMATE Cash Card will be activated once the information provided by the Customer is validated by BRB, who shall not be liable for any failure of the Customer not being approved a CASHMATE Cash Card due to the failure of the Customer to provide the required information. 3. OWNERSHIP OF THE CASHMATE CASH CARD. The Customer’s registered information and valid identification presented are presumed to be correct, true and valid. The Customer shall be responsible for the security of the CASHMATE Cash Card and Personal Identification Number (PIN) once assigned. The Customer agrees to immediately sign the signature portion located on the back of the Cash Card and undertakes that the Card will be securely kept at all times.

4. RELEVANT CASHMATE CASH CARD FEES. The Customer agrees to pay fees, applicable taxes and other charges related to the use of the CASHMATE Cash Card, as provided by BRB, such as, but not limited to, ATM, Cashmate Tellering and POS withdrawal, balance inquiry, and loading fees. Fees and other charges shall be inclusive of all applicable Philippine taxes and shall be debited from the Customer’s Cash Card balance. The amount of fees and charges may be revised from time to time, as BRB may, in their absolute opinion, deem necessary. Please refer to the latest posted cash card fees.

5. LOADING OF THE CASHMATE CASH CARD. The CASHMATE Cash card is reloadable upon purchase. The Customer must load a peso value onto the CASHMATE Cash Card prior to usage. Loading can be done at the BRB Head Office and Partner Agent Branches. The minimum allowable load amount is PHP 100 Per Customer, the aggregate load limit is PHP 100,000 per calendar month. The loading service fee will be deducted immediately from every successful loaded transaction amount. All load transactions shall be payable in cash. No other modes of payment such as checks shall be accepted for loading. The aggregate load limit per Customer/month is based on Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) Circular 649 (Guidelines Governing the Issuance of Electronic Money) and such other guidelines as may be issued by the BSP from time to time.

6. REDEMPTION OF LOAD. The CASHMATE Cash Card may be redeemed at face value. It shall not earn interest, rewards and other similar incentives convertible to cash, nor can it be purchased at a discount.

7. USE OF THE CASHMATE CASH CARD. You can use your CASHMATE Cash Card to withdraw cash from any BancNet ATM and POS Cash-out or purchase goods and services at any POS Terminal connected to BancNet.

8. ATM CAPTURE. In the event that a non-personalized CASHMATE Cash Card is captured in any BancNet ATM, the card will not be returned but destroyed by the bank that captured it. This is a BancNet policy for unnamed CASHMATE Cash Cards. Hence, the Customer must report this immediately to the Customer Care Hotline, and he/she must buy a new CASHMATE Cash Card. Thereafter, the Customer shall call the hotline again to request for the transfer of the remaining load from the old Cash Card to the new one.

9. ATM ISSUES – NO-CASH & SHORT-CASH DISPENSING. The Customer must report the incident/s of such withdrawals to the BRB Customer Care Hotline. BRB shall conduct an investigation based on the Customer’s report. If details of the report match BRB’s record of the transaction, the money shall be credited back to the Customer’s Cash Card account.

10.TRANSACTION DISPUTE. The Customer or his/her representative must, at all times, ask for the confirmation receipt as proof of every successful transaction and as reference in case of a dispute.

11.LOST OR STOLEN CARD. In the event that the CASHMATE Cash Card is lost or stolen, the loss or theft must be immediately reported by the Customer to BRB’s Customer Care Hotline (02) 8478-6615, (02) 8478-6618 or (02) 8852-1454 and (Globe) 0916-6243607 (Smart) 0998-3268399. All transactions are considered valid prior to BRB’s receipt of advice of a card’s loss or theft, including, but not limited to, fraudulent transactions and/or forged signature. Blocking or cancellation of the CASHMATE Cash Card shall be processed only upon verbal notification, proper customer authentication and actual recording thereof by BRB.

12.VALIDITY AND EXPIRY OF THE CASHMATE Cash Card. Unless earlier terminated by BRB, or voluntarily cancelled or returned by the Customer, the CASHMATE Cash Card shall be valid from the day of issuance up to the last day of the month indicated on the expiry date (MM/YY). There is no option for renewal of the Cash Card. Therefore, upon expiry, the Customer must purchase a new Cash Card to continue enjoying the CASHMATE Cash Card services. The Customer shall not use the Cash Card after its expiry date, cancellation, suspension, termination, withdrawal, or permit other persons to use the Cash Card for any reason whatsoever.

13.DISHONOR OF CARD. The Customer agrees to hold BRB, its officers and employees, free and harmless from any liability arising from the failure of any accredited establishment, BRB or third-party merchant to honor the Cash Card, or as a result of any defect with the Cash Card.
